个人简介

Dawei Cheng is currently an assistant professor appointed at Department of Computer Science and Technology in Tongji University, Shanghai, China. I specialize in data mining, machine learning, and deep learning. Now I mainly focus on graph learning in compext financial networks and knowledge graphs.

Prior to now, I was a postdoctoral associate at Center for Brain-like Computing and Machine Intelligence (BCMI), Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) , China. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D degree in computer science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Prof. Liqing Zhang and bachelor degree from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA) in China.
